Our Process

Structured steps. Clean decisions.

Our recruitment approach is designed to reduce uncertainty for both clients and professionals. Each step builds clarity so the final choice feels confident and well-supported.

The 5-step recruitment process

From assignment review to weekly evaluation.

  1. 1. Position / assignment review
    We get to know the client personally and learn about the organization and the open vacancy. We lead with questions to pinpoint the preferred experience, education, skills, and competencies—alongside company culture, communication style, team composition, and more.

    These essential factors help us select the best-matched and available professionals.
  2. 2. Position refining, introduction, and selection
    We summarize step 1 and formulate the recruitment text to share within our network. Our #1 priority is to find the right professional for the client.
  3. 3. Candidate proposal to client
    After finding the right candidates, we complete CV selection and an introductory meeting, then present the candidates to the client.
  4. 4. Agreement with client
    The client decides who they want to proceed with for interviews and ultimately who they want to choose.
  5. 5. Evaluation
    We run regular weekly check-ins during the initial trial period. We assess satisfaction of both the client and the professional, the quality of our services, and the professional’s performance.

Clean recruitment. Clear outcomes.
