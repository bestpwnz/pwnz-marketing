For Clients

Hiring should feel clear and controlled.

We start with a deep assignment review, then refine the position and shortlist professionals who match the role and your culture. The outcome is a calm, confident hiring decision, not a flood of mismatched CVs.

Discuss a position See the full process

What you can expect

A consistent experience from first conversation through weekly evaluation.

Assignment clarity

We lead with questions to pinpoint experience, education, skills, competencies, and also culture fit and team dynamics.

Focused selection

We refine the position text and share it within our network with one priority: the right professional for your needs.

Client control

You decide who to interview and who to hire. We support you with structured proposals and clear communication.

Success evaluation

After selection, we run weekly check-ins during the initial trial period to assess satisfaction, performance, and service quality for both you and the professional.

Ongoing support and upskilling

Beyond the trial period, we help identify and facilitate regular upskilling opportunities for your professionals to ensure continued growth and alignment with evolving role requirements.

Quick FAQ

A few common questions from hiring teams.

What do you need to start?

A short call to understand the assignment, success profile, and team context. We’ll guide the questions.

How do candidate proposals work?

After CV selection and an introductory meeting, we present a shortlist with relevant context for decision-making.

Do you support onboarding?

We run weekly check-ins during the initial period to ensure satisfaction and performance stay aligned as part of the initial onboarding of the professional.
