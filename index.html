Our process

Five steps, from understanding the assignment to evaluation after hire.

  1. Position / assignment review
    We learn the role, organization, and success profile.
  2. Refining & selection
    We craft the recruitment text and begin targeted outreach.
  3. Candidate proposal
    Shortlist, introductory meetings, then present candidates.
  4. Agreement
    The client selects who to interview and hire.
  5. Evaluation
    Weekly check-ins during the initial trial period.
